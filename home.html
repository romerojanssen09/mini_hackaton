Fun Facts About Wildlife

Elephant Memory

Elephants have excellent memories and can remember other elephants and locations for decades. They can also recognize themselves in mirrors!

Learn More

Hummingbird Speed

Hummingbirds can flap their wings up to 80 times per second and are the only birds that can fly backwards!

Learn More

Octopus Intelligence

Octopuses have nine brains - one central brain and eight additional brains in each tentacle. They can also change color to blend with their surroundings!

Learn More

Spider Silk

Spider silk is five times stronger than steel of the same thickness. Some spiders can produce up to seven different types of silk!

Learn More

Penguin Parenting

Emperor penguins are the only animals that breed during the Antarctic winter. Males incubate the eggs while females hunt for food!

Learn More

Dolphin Sleep

Dolphins sleep with only half their brain at a time, keeping the other half awake to breathe and watch for predators!
